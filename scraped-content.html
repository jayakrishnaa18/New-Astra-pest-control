Our other Cleaning Services

Carpet Steam Cleaning

The carpet steam cleaning method also known as hot water extraction uses the power of steam to deep clean your carpets. In this, a hose nozzle is used to spray heated water with dissolved cleaning solutions directly onto the carpet. Then innovative truck mounted steam machines vacuums the liquid. It ensures removal of any of the dirt and grime trapped into the carpet fibres. This method is one of the most recommended carpet cleaning methods by carpet manufacturers across the globe. If you have deeply soiled carpets, then this method is the best to get rid of that stubborn soil.

Carpet Dry Cleaning

Carpet dry cleaning is also known as no-moisture or “very low moisture” carpet cleaning technique. Specialised and advanced truck mounted machines sprays dry shampoos on the carpet to clean the carpets. It helps to get rid of dirt, stains, and allergens from the carpet fibers. However, this method doesn’t work for heavily soiled carpets.

Frequently Asked Questions:

When the carpet becomes safe to use after cleaning?

Once the cleaning is done your carpet will be somewhat damp and generally, it takes around 3-8 hours for a carpet to be fully dried. However, we recommend that you use or walk over the carpet only after 24 hours. You can also switch on an external fan or air drier to ensure fast drying and use the carpet within 12 hours of the cleaning.

Why should we hire professionals for carpet cleaning?

Professional cleaners have experience in the carpet cleaning industry and hence they clean the carpets in a better way. So, hiring them is a good decision. Professional carpet cleaning teams have innovative cleaning equipment and supplies to ensure thorough cleaning of your carpets. They are able to remove even the dirt and gripe that is trapped deep inside the carpet fibres. They have expertise in cleaning carpets and hence they can clean the carpets in a better way.

How long is the steam cleaning process?

Steam carpet cleaning is the most recommended carpet cleaning method. Also known as hot water extraction method this method effectively removes the dirt and grime from deep inside your carpet fibers. It takes around 4-6 hours depending upon the size of the carpet for the carpet steam cleaning process to get completed. The process completion duration also depends on how dirty is the carpet.

Will the furniture be moved for the carpet cleaning service?

If you have placed some heavy furniture on the carpet, then it will not be possible for us to move it. Hence, in order to get your carpet cleaned, you have to remove any furniture placed on it. Anything placed on the carpet doesn’t allow to operate the cleaning tools and equipment efficiently, hence reducing the quality of carpet cleaning. So, when you book carpet cleaning services, you should ensure to move anything placed on the carpets.

How often should I hire professional carpet cleaning services?

Carpets go through so much daily. They are walked on, things spill over them, pets sit on them and so on. It makes them a breeding ground for bacteria and viruses, so you need to get them cleaned professionally at least twice a year apart from doing regular vacuuming. It will keep your carpets looking as brand new and also prolong their life.

Do you offer a carpet dry cleaning service?

Yes, we also offer carpet dry cleaning service. We offer carpet stain removal, sanitising, deodorising and carpet steam cleaning services at best and competitive prices. If you are looking to book quality carpet cleaning, then you should call us or visit our website at the earliest.

Which is the best carpet cleaning method?

Carpet steam cleaning is the best carpet cleaning method. It uses truck-mounted machines and quality cleaning supplies to remove the dirt, stains, allergens and grime which are deeply stuck inside the carpet fibres. This method is also called hot water extraction method and uses the power of steam to thoroughly clean the carpets. Further, the carpets usually get completely dried within 3-6 hours once the carpet cleaning is done. So, you can use it within a very short time after cleaning. This carpet cleaning method doesn’t affect the quality of the carpet and leave them looking as brand new.

Do you also provide same day carpet cleaning services?

Yes, we do. If you are looking for same day carpet cleaning, then we can help you. You just need to book our services online or on call and tell us your needs. We will reach your place within a few hours of booking.

Can you remove all kinds of stains from your carpets?

We try to get rid of even the most difficult to remove stains from your carpets without damaging your carpet fibers. However, there are some types of stubborn stains that can’t be get rid of. So, before promising anything, we check the nature of stains and then try to remove the stains completely.

Are your services safe for kids and pets?

We are a customer-centric carpet cleaning business and hence we are very much concerned about the well-being of our clients and their family. So, we use quality and safe cleaning products which are free from harmful chemicals to clean your carpets. Our chosen cleaning products are safe for your pets and kids.
